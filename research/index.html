I'm interested in low dimensional topology, manifolds, knot theory, embedding spaces, and cobordisms.

Thesis

My thesis constructed 3-parameter families of embedded arcs in 4-manifolds. The main tool I use to study them are finite type invariants coming from Embedding Calculus. I also use notation inpired by cubical ω groupoids.

Other Papers

Another current research project deals with cobordism categories and TQFTs. We study the category of nested cobordisms between nested manifolds.

  • Nested cobordisms, Cyl-objects and Temperley-Lieb algebras, with M. Calle, R. Hoekzema, L. Murray, N. Pacheco-Tallaj, C. Rovi, Mar 2024 [arxiv]

As an undergraduate, I worked on research projects on Hyperbolic knots, Schur functions, cyclic group actions on cyclic codes, and graph and matroid theory.

  • Support Equalities Among Ribbon Schur Functions, with M. Gaetz, W. Hardt, The Electronic Journal of Combinatorics , Volume 26 Issue 3. Sep 2019 [arxiv]

  • Cyclic Sieving for Cyclic Codes, with A. Mason, V. Reiner, Finite Fields and Their Applications. 73. Aug 2021 [arxiv]
